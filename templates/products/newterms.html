DEFINITIONS OF TERMS

1. AUCTION

The lowest unique bid format used by QuickBid in order to determine the winning bid.

2. BID

An amount in Kenya Shillings that a Bidder has entered or intends to enter into the Auction for a specific Item.

3. BID ENTRY FEE

The amount in Kenya Shillings (Ksh) sent via Mpesa in order to place a Bid.

4. BIDDER

The User of the service that has placed or intends to place a Bid.

5. ITEM

The product(s) or other worthy inventory that is the focus of a specific Auction.

6. NON-UNIQUE BID

A Bid that has been placed on a specific Auction by a Bidder where at least one other Bidder has also placed an identical Bid on the same Auction.

7. RRP (Recommended Retail Price)

The manufacturer’s recommended retail price for a specific Item within Our Auctions.

8. UNIQUE BID

A Bid that has been placed on a specific Auction by a Bidder where no other Bidder has placed an identical Bid on the same Auction.
